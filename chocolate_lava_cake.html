Chocolate lava cake is a decadent dessert that combines the richness of a chocolate cake with a gooey, molten center. To craft it with hygiene as a priority and quality as the weapon, start by using premium ingredients—high-quality dark chocolate, fresh eggs, and pure butter. Maintain a clean workstation and follow strict food safety measures to ensure freshness and purity. The secret to perfection lies in precise baking; the edges should be firm, while the center remains molten. Serve immediately, pairing it with fresh cream or vanilla ice cream, delivering a luxurious treat that’s as safe as it is indulgent.
