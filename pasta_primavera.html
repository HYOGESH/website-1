Pasta Primavera is a vibrant dish that combines pasta with fresh, sautéed vegetables and a light, flavorful sauce. To make it with hygiene as a priority and quality as the weapon, start by selecting the freshest seasonal vegetables and high-quality pasta. Wash and prepare the vegetables meticulously, ensuring cleanliness at every step. Use olive oil, garlic, and fresh herbs to enhance the flavors naturally, avoiding artificial additives. Cook the pasta to a perfect al dente texture and toss it gently with the sautéed vegetables. Serve immediately for a wholesome, colorful, and delightfully fresh meal that embodies both safety and taste.
